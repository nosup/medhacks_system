Travel Reimbursement Application

National and International travel reimbursement applications are due July 20th. Regional travel reimbursements are due August 14th.

Because you requested a travel reimbursement, you will not receive a decision to attend MedHacks 2018 until you submit your travel reimbursement.

Reimbursement Types:

  • No selection- Local: within 25 miles of Johns Hopkins Homewood Campus (for example, UMD College Park). You will not be reimbursed, but if there is enough interest from a local college, the college will be eligible for a free bus. There are also a number of free public transportation options for local participants.
  • Selection: Regional: includes the Northeast and some parts of the South and Midwest where traveling is less than $50 (i.e. BoltBus and MegaBus). You will only be reimbursed up to $50 and no more.
  • Selection: National: includes locations where costs to travel to MedHacks significantly exceeds $50. Reimbursement amounts will vary depending on region from which a student is flying.
    • Midwest (up to $100)
    • West (up to $250)
  • Selection: International: includes locations out of the United States. International participants will receive up to $250 on a case by case basis.
